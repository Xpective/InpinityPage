Inpinity Collaboration

Collaboration & Partnerships

Inpinity is built to collaborate: creators, communities, and projects can plug into the Pyramid. Partnerships can unlock special resources, partner blocks, and future Inpinity City utilities — with a path into the upcoming UE5 game.

Central Resource

Oil at the center

Oil is one of the core strategic resources in the Inpinity ecosystem. It represents movement, energy, expansion, and long-term utility. As the ecosystem grows, it becomes a central building block for gameplay, production, upgrades, and the future of Inpinity City.

🧬 What a Partnership Means

We can integrate partner communities directly into the Pyramid economy.

Partner token / NFT → becomes a special resource (or unlocks one) used in gameplay, protection, boosts, and future city upgrades.

Open for Partners

📦 Partner Resources (ERC1155)

Resources are on-chain and visible, usable and tradable. A partnership can introduce:

• new resource type
• special “partner-only” drops
• city crafting ingredients for Inpinity City

Roadmap + Live Core

⭐ Partner Blocks

Partner blocks can be marked as special and linked to collaborations.

Those blocks can unlock unique rewards, airdrops, and special production rules through events and integrations.
