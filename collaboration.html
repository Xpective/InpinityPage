Inpinity Collaboration

Build with Inpinity

Development, art, resources, and ecosystem expansion around Inpinity City.

Inpinity is designed as a growing ecosystem where creators, developers, strategists, and builders can contribute to a shared vision. Collaboration is not only about promotion — it is about shaping systems, assets, mechanics, and long-term value together.

Central Resource

Oil at the center

Oil stands as one of the key strategic resources in the ecosystem and represents energy, growth, movement, and expansion. It connects resource production, crafting potential, and the long-term vision behind Inpinity City.

Why collaborate with Inpinity?

The ecosystem brings together game systems, resource mechanics, NFTs, strategy, and future city-building structures in one evolving framework.

Development

Collaborate on frontend systems, smart contract integration, gameplay loops, UI improvements, dashboards, map logic, and ecosystem tools.

Art & Assets

Help shape the visual identity of resources, city systems, items, block presentation, and future world-building assets.

Economy & Resources

Contribute to balancing, exchange ideas, resource flows, progression loops, and meaningful interactions between assets and strategy.

Community & Vision

Join in building the culture, direction, partnerships, and expansion paths around Inpinity and Inpinity City.

Inpinity City

Inpinity City is the collaborative layer where shared structures, resources, and strategic development come together.

Shared construction

Inpinity City is intended as a space for collective growth, where systems, buildings, and long-term expansion can emerge through contribution and coordination.

Resource-driven expansion

Core resources like Oil, Lemons, Iron, Gold, and others can become the foundation for city structures, upgrades, and advanced strategic systems.

Future gameplay layer

The city layer can grow into a broader system of ownership, collaboration, crafting, exchange, defense, and coordinated progression.

Who can collaborate?

Inpinity is open to different kinds of contributors who want to help shape the ecosystem.

Developers

Frontend, contracts, integrations, UI systems, tools, and game-support logic.

Designers & Artists

Resources, items, city visuals, icons, branding, and ecosystem identity.

Strategic Partners

Community growth, aligned projects, content collaboration, and ecosystem support.
