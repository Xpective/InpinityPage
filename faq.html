General Questions

What is Inpinity?

Inpinity is a decentralized ecosystem bridging blockchain technology, gaming, and digital advertising. It's a community-driven platform for game developers and enthusiasts to create, share, and grow. The native token $INPI powers staking, governance, in-game utilities, and more. For details, check our whitepaper.

Token & Staking

What is the $INPI token?

$INPI is the native utility token of Inpinity. Total supply is 1 billion tokens. It is used for staking, governance, rewards, in-game purchases, and will generate the $ADVERT sub-token for advertising utilities. New contract address: 0x232FB12582ac10d5fAd97e9ECa22670e8Ba67d0D (the old address 0x15ac8e2c38f0c644123e61011e7113de56038453 is deprecated due to ToshiMart issues).

We are currently conducting a presale! Visit https://inpinity.online/token/ to participate. The presale runs until March 5, 2026, 20:20 UTC. After that, $INPI will be listed on Uniswap (Base network). Always verify the contract address from our official channels.

When does staking launch?

Staking is scheduled for Q2 2026. The staking contract is already deployed at 0xF8c74E4fEe036C91228268E804f83eD8f55a6524 and will be activated soon. You will be able to stake your $INPI tokens to earn passive rewards.

What is "Judgment Day"?

"Judgment Day" is a key milestone event that unlocks the team & advisor tokens (20% of supply). Until then, those tokens are fully locked to ensure trust and long-term commitment. The exact definition of Judgment Day will be communicated to the community (e.g., reaching a certain number of holders, launching the game, etc.).

How do I stake my tokens?

Once staking is live (Q2 2026), you will need to connect your wallet (e.g., MetaMask) to the Inpinity dApp and choose a staking period. Rewards accumulate based on the amount and duration staked. Step-by-step guides will be provided.
