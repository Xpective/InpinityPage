FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is Inpinity?

Inpinity is a decentralized ecosystem bridging blockchain technology, gaming, and digital advertising. It's a community-driven platform for game developers and enthusiasts to create, share, and grow. The native token $INPI powers staking, governance, in-game utilities, and more. For details, check our whitepaper.

How can I join the community?

You can join our Discord server: https://discord.gg/95yj6Eg6, follow us on Twitter @unxpective, or join our Telegram group: https://t.me/+1hl5Ou-pGChhYmUy. All links are also available on our Contact page.

Where can I find the whitepaper?

The Inpinity whitepaper is available on GitHub: Download PDF. You can also access it via the "Whitepaper" link in the navigation menu.

Token & Staking

What is the $INPI token?

$INPI is the native utility token of Inpinity. Total supply is 1 billion tokens. It is used for staking, governance, rewards, in-game purchases, and will generate the $ADVERT sub-token for advertising utilities. Contract address: 0x15ac8e2c38f0c644123e61011e7113de56038453.

How can I buy $INPI?

During the initial launch (Q1 2026), $INPI will be available via our presale page: https://inpinity.online/token/. After that, it will be listed on decentralized exchanges (DEXs) like Uniswap. Keep an eye on our announcements for official contract addresses to avoid scams.

When does staking launch?

Staking is scheduled for Q2 2026. You will be able to stake your $INPI tokens to earn passive rewards. More details will be released closer to the launch.

What is "Judgment Day"?

"Judgment Day" is a key milestone event that unlocks the team & advisor tokens (20% of supply). Until then, those tokens are fully locked to ensure trust and long-term commitment. The exact definition of Judgment Day will be communicated to the community (e.g., reaching a certain number of holders, launching the game, etc.).

How do I stake my tokens?

Once staking is live (Q2 2026), you will need to connect your wallet (e.g., MetaMask) to the Inpinity dApp and choose a staking period. Rewards accumulate based on the amount and duration staked. Step-by-step guides will be provided.

Game & NFTs

What games are available?

The first mini-game is planned for Q4 2026. A full-fledged game with NFT integration will follow. Stay tuned for updates on our social channels.

How do I get NFTs?

The first NFT collection "Inpinity Genesis" will be introduced in Q4 2026. Holders of $INPI may get exclusive minting rights. NFTs will be tradeable on our upcoming marketplace (Q1 2027).

Can I use NFTs in the game?

Yes, by Q3 2027, NFTs will have full in-game utility (playable characters, skins, power-ups). More details will be revealed as development progresses.
