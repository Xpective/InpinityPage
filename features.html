Partnerships in Inpinity are not just logos or tweets. We turn partner projects into real gameplay utility inside the Pi-Phi Pyramid: resources, drops, special blocks, and later Infinite City upgrades + the full UE5 game.

1) What a Partnership means in Inpinity

A partnership is a plug-in layer for our economy. Your project can become an on-chain material and a strategic gameplay lane. Players don’t just “see” your brand — they use it (earn → trade → consume).

Core promise: Partnership utility remains relevant long-term because it ties into farming, raids, protection meta, and later city progression.

2) Partner Resources (ERC1155) — the clean standard

All resources in Inpinity live inside our ERC1155 ResourceToken. A partner can be represented as a new resource id (or a special variation) with clear rules: who can obtain it, how, and what it is used for.

Integration models:

  • Partner Token → Resource (mapped to a resource id; used as crafting/upgrade material)
  • Partner NFT → Catalyst (boost drop chance, unlock a special lane, or reduce raid risk)
  • Event Resource (seasonal, tournament, limited supply)
  • Cosmetic Lane (non-pay-to-win value; skins, badges, city visuals later)

Why players love it:

  • Resources are visible in dashboard + tradable like real items
  • Partner drops feel like “rare loot” inside a strategy meta
  • Communities merge through shared goals and events
  • Long-term sink: Infinite City consumes special mats

3) Partner Blocks — co-op mechanics, not marketing

Partnerships can activate special partner blocks inside the Pyramid. This makes collaborations strategic: a block can become an event anchor, a drop trigger, or a utility node for the entire community.

  • Event anchors: partner blocks trigger season rewards / community raids
  • Quest logic: own partner asset + own block → unlock drops or boosts
  • Rare lane: some resources are partnership-only (e.g., Copper/Obsidian style)
Balance: We can configure every partnership to avoid pay-to-win. The best lane is “utility + identity”, not “power inflation”.

4) The vision — Infinite City + UE5

Partnerships become even more valuable when Inpinity City arrives: special resources can be consumed to unlock city buildings, upgrades, boosters, and defensive layers. After that, the UE5 version turns the on-chain pyramid into a playable world economy.

  • Infinite City: resource sinks, buildings, upgrades, long-term progression
  • UE5 Game: your blocks become the strategic layer for gameplay and crafting
  • Partner identity: partner resources become “real materials” for real content

5) Partnership process (fast + realistic)

  • Step 1: choose model (resource / catalyst / event / cosmetics)
  • Step 2: define rules (fairness, unlock, max supply, time windows)
  • Step 3: ship UI integration (icons, balances, drop display)
  • Step 4: activate partner blocks + announce together
  • Step 5: extend into Infinite City + UE5 utility
What we need: contract address(es), brand asset pack (icons), and agreement on drop rules / usage rules.
