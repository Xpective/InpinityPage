How To / FAQ V6 + Mercenary V4

A quick guide to the main gameplay loops inside the dashboard.

What's new in V6 + Mercenary V4?
  • Enhanced claim and block state flow
  • Pirate and farming boosts with clearer state handling
  • Better claim preview flow
  • Mercenary V4 slot system with permanent slot unlocks
  • Duration-based protection pricing from 1 to 7 days
  • Immediate points on protection start plus completion rewards
  • Attack execution moved to the Pyramid Map for better stability
How does minting work?

Choose coordinates, select a payment option, and mint a Pyramid Block NFT. After minting, the block can later be revealed and used for farming, defense, and attacks.

What does revealing a block do?

Revealing unlocks the block's hidden state and exposes its rarity-related attributes. That makes the block fully usable in the wider game flow.

How does Mercenary Protection V4 work?

Each wallet starts with 1 protection slot. You can permanently unlock Slot 2 and Slot 3 by burning resources.

Protection can be set for 1 to 7 days:

  • 1–2 days = 20% protection
  • 3–5 days = 30% protection
  • 6–7 days = 50% protection

Resource costs stay fixed by duration. INPI payments receive the rank-based discount.

How do Defender Points work?

Defender Points grow through active protection usage. Short protection gives fewer points, longer protection gives more, and successful finished protection adds extra rewards.

  • 1–2 days protection start = +2 points
  • 3–5 days protection start = +5 points
  • 6–7 days protection start = +10 points
  • Completed protection adds more points depending on duration

Higher ranks improve your INPI discount and strengthen your defender profile.

How do slot unlocks work?

Slot 2 unlock: 1000 Oil, 500 Lemons, 100 Iron
Slot 3 unlock: 500 Oil, 100 Lemons, 100 Iron, 50 Gold, 25 Crystal, 5 Mysterium

These unlocks are permanent per wallet.

How do attacks work now?

The heavy attack flow has been moved to the Pyramid Map to keep the Game page stable. Use the map for target selection and attack execution.

Inside the Game dashboard, you can still:

  • review your active attacks,
  • open ready attacks in the map,
  • cancel pending attacks,
  • and buy Pirate Boost for a selected attacker block.
What are boosts?

Farming Boost costs 100 INPI per day and increases production by 25%. The contract allows up to 10 days, while the farming window itself lasts 7 days.

Pirate Boost costs 100 PIT per day and adds +25 percentage points to the steal rate before protection penalties are applied. The contract allows up to 10 days.

What is cleanup for?

Cleanup allows expired or stale protection entries to be cleared. Depending on your rank, cleanup can reward materials such as Iron, Copper, or Obsidian.

What is the exchange for?

The exchange area lets you view the current INPI ↔ PIT relation and swap between both sides depending on pool state and contract availability.
