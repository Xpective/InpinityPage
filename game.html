🎮 How to Play

Move your mouse or finger left/right to control the collector paddle at the bottom. Golden rings (data packets) appear at the top – catch them with your paddle to earn points. Each catch increases your score by 10. The game speeds up as you progress!

Every 50 points – the speed increases, and the collector's AI opponent gets faster too. How long can you keep up?

This is a simplified demo. The full Inpinity game will feature blockchain integration, NFT assets, and a rich gaming economy.
