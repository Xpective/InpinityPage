🎮 Inpinity On-Chain Game Ecosystem

Inpinity is building a playable blockchain economy on Base. You mint a Pyramid Block NFT with fixed coordinates, reveal it to unlock Pi/Phi rarity, then farm resources, defend with mercenaries, and raid other blocks using pirates. Everything is verifiable on-chain and shown live in the Game and Map.

1) Mint a Block

Mint a Pyramid Block NFT with fixed Row/Col.

Start here: mint.html

2) Reveal (Pi/Phi)

Reveal your block to unlock Pi/Phi digits (rarity & production).

Do it in: game.html or map.html

3) Farming (V3)

Start FarmingV3 → accumulate ERC-1155 resources → claim anytime.

Farming engine is V3.

4) Protect & Attack

Hire mercenaries, start pirate attacks, then execute when ready.

Attacks run via PiratesV3.

Welcome to Inpinity

Unite, Create, Experience. Inpinity is not just a platform, but a home for visionary game designers and developers. We combine community-building with real on-chain gameplay loops: mint, reveal, farm, protect, raid — and later integrate into UE5.

Our Vision

We want a game world shaped by creators: a platform where blockchain isn’t a barrier, but a gameplay engine. The Pyramid system is the first live module — and it expands into future UE5 exploration, crafting and strategy layers.

Why Inpinity?

Community: With us, community comes first. Collaborate, share ideas, and grow with other talented individuals.

On-chain gameplay: Real contracts already live (NFT, farming, pirates, mercenaries, resources).

Experience: Transparent, verifiable systems that evolve into a full game experience over time.

Join us!

Whether you’re an experienced developer or a newcomer: mint your first block, start farming, build protection, launch raids — and be part of the next stage when the UE5 gameplay layer arrives.
