Inpinity is building a playable on-chain world on Base: you mint a Pyramid Block NFT with fixed coordinates (Row/Col), reveal your Pi/Phi digits (rarity), then farm resources, defend with mercenaries, and attack other blocks with pirates. Welcome to Inpinity

Unite, Create, Experience. Inpinity is a home for visionary builders: game designers, developers, artists, and on-chain pioneers. Our goal is simple: make web3 gameplay feel like a real game — not a spreadsheet.

How the Pyramid Game works

1) Mint a Block NFT (Row/Col) on Base → your coordinates are permanent.

2) Reveal your block to unlock Pi/Phi digits → rarity → production profile.

3) Start Farming on FarmingV3 → claim ERC-1155 resources over time.

4) Defend your block with Mercenaries → reduces pirate success.

5) Attack other blocks via PiratesV3 → wait until ready → execute → resources move on-chain.

You can do everything from Game and Map.
