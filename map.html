Map FAQ

Quick overview of what the map is for.

What is the Pyramid Map?

The Pyramid Map is the visual overview of the Inpinity block structure. It helps you inspect positions, ownership, and the broader world layout of the ecosystem.

Can I use this map before owning a block?

Yes. The map can still be explored as a world overview, while ownership and interaction become more relevant once you mint blocks.

Does the map connect to future gameplay?

Yes. The map is intended to become a stronger gameplay layer over time, especially for block identity, territory logic, and future game-oriented modules.
